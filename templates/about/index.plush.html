Our Manifesto

The status quo is stagnation. Most companies operate in maintenance mode—optimizing what exists, never questioning what could be. Internal innovation dies in endless meetings, buried under "not our core business" or "we'll just buy a tool for that."

We believe every company deserves to be 1% better every day. Not through top-down mandates or expensive consultants, but by empowering the people who know the problems best: your team.

Internal hackathons unlock builder culture. When employees are given time, structure, and recognition to prototype ideas—whether it's automating a painful process, testing a new product concept, or upskilling in emerging tech—the entire organization moves faster. Teams stop waiting for permission. Innovation becomes routine, not rare.

Why Companies Struggle

Running internal hackathons shouldn't be hard, but it often is. Companies face:

  • Complex tooling — Expensive platforms designed for public events, not internal workflows
  • Logistics overhead — Tracking teams, scheduling, scoring, and follow-up becomes a full-time job
  • No clear outcomes — Ideas disappear after demo day with no path to production
  • Momentum loss — One-off events that never become repeatable culture

Our Solution

This service is simple, self-hosted, and built for internal use. Deploy it once. Run hackathons as often as you want. Track participants, teams, submissions, and outcomes in one place. Let your builders build.

When anyone in your organization can prototype an idea, present it to leadership, and get green-lit for further development—you've created a competitive advantage that no vendor can sell you.
